About JMR Turris Fortis

JMR Turris Fortis Realty Corporation is a Philippine-based real estate company focused on developing and selling high-quality residential and commercial properties. We are committed to building and selling modern, sustainable, and well-designed communities that meet the evolving needs of Filipino families and businesses in South Luzon.

Guided by integrity, innovation, and a passion for excellence, JMR Turris Fortis aims to create and sell spaces that offer comfort, functionality, and long-term value. Whether it's a family home, a mixed-use development, or a commercial space, we ensure each project reflects strong design, quality construction, and smart urban planning.

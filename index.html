About JMR Turris Fortis

JMR Turris Fortis Realty Corporation is a Philippine-based real estate company focused on brokering high-quality residential and commercial properties. The company is committed to building and selling modern, sustainable, and well-designed communities that meet the evolving needs of Filipino families and businesses in South Luzon and Metro Manila.

Guided by integrity, innovation, and a passion for excellence, JMR Turris Fortis aims to create and sell spaces that offer comfort, functionality, and long-term value. Whether it's a family home, a mixed-use development, or a commercial space, the company ensures each project reflects strong design, quality construction, and smart urban planning.

Founded and Headed by Mr. Jasper M. Robles, REB, CTEP, RFC, REP, AWP, AEPP with a dynamic team of passionate Property Investment Consultants, we endeavor to provide utmost service to all our clientele.
